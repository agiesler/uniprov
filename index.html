Abstract

This document explains the ontology for UniProv enabling provenance management in the UNICORE federation system.

Introduction

The UniProv ontology extends the W3C PROV data model and the workflow extension PROVONE by defining a vocabulary for UNICORE (http://unicore.eu) specific job and workflow logic.
